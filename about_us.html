OUR STORY

In 1996 Our Butchery started out as a small butcher shop on London Street. Me and my brother valued hard-work, honesty and mastering the craft of butchery.

Our Butchery was founded on a sound set of values & foundations that are as relevant now as they were 25 years ago. Trust, honesty, integrity & a fierce passion for quality. Me and my brother are as focused on these core beliefs now more than ever.

Our family owned & operated business has been at the epicentre of change, innovation & a true belief not only in the art of butchery but also the need for innovation in our amazing industry.

I has always believed in giving the first & most enthusiastic nod to the primary producers that are the heart & soul of our industry. We understand we are nothing without what they do and strive every day to do justice to their hard work and passion.

Our Butchery is now giving the public rare access to our impressive range of award-winning meats. That means, you and your family can now enjoy products usually destined for the best restaurants in London.

This passion and dedication have culminated in Our Butchery becoming London's leading premium food retailer.
